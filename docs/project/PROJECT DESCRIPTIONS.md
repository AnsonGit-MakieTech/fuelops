# Project Description: FuelOps Station Tracker

FuelOps Station Tracker is a web-based gasoline station management system designed to help station owners monitor daily operations, fuel sales, inventory movement, expenses, and profitability in one centralized platform.

The system focuses on tracking premium and diesel sales through daily pump meter readings, allowing the business to calculate liters sold, expected sales, actual collections, and cash shortages or overages. It also records fuel refills, supplier deliveries, fuel costs, and tank inventory to help identify possible fuel loss, stock variance, or operational leakage.

Aside from sales and inventory, the platform includes expense tracking for salaries, bills, calibration, fuel purchases, maintenance, government fees, license renewals, and other station-related costs. These records are used to generate daily, monthly, and profit-based reports that give owners a clear view of their real business performance.

The goal of the system is to help gasoline station owners move away from manual notebooks or scattered Excel files and provide them with a reliable digital tool for monitoring sales, controlling expenses, detecting cash or fuel discrepancies, and making better business decisions.

FuelOps Station Tracker will initially be built as a responsive web application that can be accessed from desktop, tablet, or mobile devices. Staff can encode daily sales and expenses, while owners and managers can review reports, approve records, monitor inventory, and track overall profitability.

In future versions, the system can be expanded into a full SaaS platform for multiple gasoline stations, with user roles, audit logs, automated alerts, report exports, renewal reminders, and AI-powered business insights.
